Classical Composers Information Page

Music has been part of human culture for thousands of years. From the 17th to the early 20th centuries, a number of famous composers who gave us some of the most recognized classical music compositions in western history. However, their work was not always restricted to symphonies. Some composers also were prolific in writing operas. This site presents a few examples of famous composers and the operas for which they are known.
